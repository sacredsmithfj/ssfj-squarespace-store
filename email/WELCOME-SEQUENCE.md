# 7-Day Email Welcome Sequence for New Newsletter Subscribers

## Day 1: Welcome & Introduce Lead Magnet
**Subject Line:** "Welcome to the Luxe Experience! 🌟"

**Body Copy:**  
Dear [Subscriber's Name],  
Welcome to our fine jewelry family! We're delighted to have you join our community of jewelry lovers. To kickstart your journey, access your exclusive lead magnet: [Lead Magnet Title]. Don't forget to download it here: [link].  
We can't wait to share our love for exquisite craftsmanship and stunning designs with you!

**Call-to-Action Button:**  
[Download Your Lead Magnet]

**Engagement Strategy:**  
Encourage replies to share what they are most excited about in jewelry.

---

## Day 2: Brand Story & Expertise
**Subject Line:** "The Heart Behind [Brand Name] 💎"

**Body Copy:**  
Hi [Subscriber's Name],  
At [Brand Name], fine jewelry is more than a passion; it's our story. Dive into our journey of craftsmanship over the years, where every piece tells a story rooted in tradition and innovation.  
Discover our rich heritage; click the link to learn more about us.

[Learn More About Our Story]

Ask subscribers to share their own jewelry stories in response.
