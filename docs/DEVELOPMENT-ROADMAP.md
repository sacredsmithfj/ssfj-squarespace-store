# 3-Month Implementation Roadmap for Fine Jewelry Studio Website Improvements

## Phase 1: Month 1 - SEO & Analytics Setup
### Week 1: Research & Planning
- **Deliverables:** Complete SEO audit
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Analyze current site SEO data.  
  2. Identify keywords and competitors.
- **Milestones:** Completion of SEO audit report.  
- **Dependencies:** None  
- **Success Metrics:** Improve site visibility score by 20%.

### Week 2: SEO Optimization
- **Deliverables:** Optimized on-page content
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Update meta tags and descriptions.  
  2. Optimize images and headers.
- **Milestones:** All pages updated.  
- **Dependencies:** Research findings from Week 1  
- **Success Metrics:** Increase organic traffic by 15%.

### Week 3: Analytics Setup
- **Deliverables:** Implement Google Analytics and SEO tools
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Set up Google Analytics tracking.  
  2. Integrate SEO tools for ongoing monitoring.
- **Milestones:** Tools operational.  
- **Dependencies:** SEO optimization completion  
- **Success Metrics:** Accuracy in data collection.

### Week 4: Review & Adjust
- **Deliverables:** Review results and adjust strategies
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Analyze data from tools.  
  2. Adjust SEO strategies based on data.
- **Milestones:** Complete review document.  
- **Dependencies:** Completion of analytics setup  
- **Success Metrics:** Improved engagement metrics.

---  

## Phase 2: Month 2 - Content & Email Launch
### Week 5: Content Strategy Development
- **Deliverables:** Content calendar
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Develop a content calendar for blogs and emails.  
  2. Plan topics that align with SEO goals.
- **Milestones:** Calendar approved.
- **Dependencies:** None  
- **Success Metrics:** Calendar delivery on time.

### Week 6: Content Creation
- **Deliverables:** Draft content for blog and emails
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Write at least 5 blog posts.  
  2. Create email templates for upcoming campaigns.
- **Milestones:** Content drafted.
- **Dependencies:** Approval of content calendar  
- **Success Metrics:** Content meets engagement goals.

### Week 7: Email Marketing Setup
- **Deliverables:** Email list and campaign setups
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Segment email list.  
  2. Configure email marketing tool.
- **Milestones:** Setup completed.
- **Dependencies:** Content creation completion  
- **Success Metrics:** Deliverability rate of >95%.

### Week 8: Review & Launch
- **Deliverables:** Finalized content and launch campaign
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Final edits on the blog posts.
  2. Launch email campaigns.
- **Milestones:** Campaign launched.
- **Dependencies:** Completion of email marketing setup  
- **Success Metrics:** Open rates >20%.

---  

## Phase 3: Month 3 - Ad Campaigns & Scaling
### Week 9: Ad Strategy Development
- **Deliverables:** Ad campaign plan
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Develop ad creative and messaging.  
  2. Research targeted demographics.
- **Milestones:** Campaign plan approved.
- **Dependencies:** Results from content campaigns  
- **Success Metrics:** Target demographic alignment.

### Week 10: Ad Creation
- **Deliverables:** Create ad assets
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Design graphics and copy for ads.  
  2. Test ad variants.
- **Milestones:** Assets completed.
- **Dependencies:** Approval of ad strategy  
- **Success Metrics:** A/B testing success rates.

### Week 11: Campaign Launch
- **Deliverables:** Launch PPC campaigns
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Set up Google Ads and Social Media Ads.  
  2. Monitor engagement and performance.
- **Milestones:** Campaign live.
- **Dependencies:** Completion of ad creation  
- **Success Metrics:** Click-through rates >10%.

### Week 12: Review & Scale
- **Deliverables:** Analyze campaign results and scale
- **Tasks:**  
  1. Review performance metrics and optimize.  
  2. Allocate budget for best-performing ads.
- **Milestones:** Scaling strategy confirmed.
- **Dependencies:** Overall campaign results  
- **Success Metrics:** ROI on ad spend above 300%.
