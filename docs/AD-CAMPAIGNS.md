# Advertising Strategy for a Luxury Fine Jewelry Studio

## Overview
This document outlines a detailed advertising strategy for a luxury fine jewelry studio targeting engaged couples, anniversary buyers, and custom jewelry clients aged 24-65 in the USA and worldwide. The strategy includes plans for Facebook Ads, Instagram Ads, and Google Ads (Search and Shopping).

---

## Target Audience
- **Engaged Couples:** 24-35 years old, planning weddings within the next year.
- **Anniversary Buyers:** 30-65 years old, looking for gifts for significant anniversaries.
- **Custom Jewelry Clients:** 24-65 years old, interested in bespoke jewelry.

---

## Budget Allocation Recommendations
- **Total Budget:** $30,000 for 3 months
  - **Facebook Ads:** $10,000
  - **Instagram Ads:** $10,000
  - **Google Ads (Search and Shopping):** $10,000

---

## Campaign Plans
### Facebook Ads
- **Ad Type:** Carousel Ads featuring different jewelry pieces.
- **Ad Copy Template:**  
  - "Experience luxury with our stunning [Jewelry Type]. Perfect for [Target Audience]."
  - Call-to-action: "Shop Now" / "Learn More"
- **Creative Direction:** High-quality images of jewelry, lifestyle images of happy couples wearing the jewelry.
- **Targeting Options:** Interests in weddings, anniversaries, and luxury goods.

### Instagram Ads
- **Ad Type:** Story Ads and feed posts featuring user-generated content.
- **Ad Copy Template:**  
  - "Make your love story last forever with our exquisite [Jewelry Type]."
- **Creative Direction:** Short videos showcasing the jewelry in different settings, testimonials from past clients.

### Google Ads (Search and Shopping)
- **Ad Type:** Text Ads for Search, Shopping Ads for online showcasing.
- **Ad Copy Template:**  
  - "Looking for the perfect [Jewelry Type]? Shop our luxury collection today!"
- **Targeting Options:** Keywords related to luxury jewelry, engagement rings, and anniversary gifts.

---

## Conversion Tracking Setup
- **Facebook:** Set up Facebook Pixel to track user interactions and conversions.
- **Google Ads:** Implement Google Analytics with conversion tracking for all ad campaigns.

---

## Month-by-Month Ad Campaign Rollout Schedule
### Month 1: Awareness
- Launch Facebook and Instagram Ads to build brand awareness.
- Begin Google Ads keyword campaigns, focus on high-intent keywords.

### Month 2: Engagement
- Retarget users who engaged with ads from Month 1.
- Increase budget for ads that performed well in Month 1.

### Month 3: Conversion
- Focus on driving sales with limited-time offers and promotions.
- Analyze performance metrics to adjust strategies as needed.

---

## Conclusion
By following this comprehensive advertising strategy, the luxury fine jewelry studio can effectively reach its target audience and increase brand awareness, engagement, and sales.
